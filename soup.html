Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This soup is delicious, and very easy to make. It is a wonderful soup to prepare for friends or neighbors that you would like to cook for.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup chopped carrots
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 8 ounces egg noodles
  • 12 cups water
  • 9 cubes chicken bouillon
  • ½ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
    1. Step 1:

      2. In a large stock pot, saute celery and onion in butter or margarine.

    2. Step 2:

      3. Add chicken, carrots, water, bouillon cubes, marjoram, black pepper, bay leaf, and parsley. Simmer for 30 minutes.

    3. Step 3:

      4. Add noodles, and simmer for 10 more minutes.
